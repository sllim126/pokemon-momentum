Fast Card-Finding Flow

1. Start with search or a screener Search when you know the card. Use Top Movers, Under the Radar, or Pullbacks when you want candidates surfaced for you.
2. Narrow with a few filters Market, product type, set, price range, and minimum score should do most of the work before we add more controls.
3. Scan results quickly The center table should answer what is moving, what looks clean, and what deserves a closer click.
4. Confirm in the right rail The detail panel should stay useful, but discovery should happen from the table first.
